Step-by-Step Guide

Steps to get the User ID and API Key for Alice Blue
  1. Log in to ANT Web Trading Platform with your phone number, password, and TOTP or mobile OTP.
  2. If prompted with a Risk Disclosure pop-up, click "Proceed."
  3. In the "Apps" tab, select "API Key," click "Copy," and paste it on your platform.
  4. For your User ID, click the profile icon, go to "Your Profile/Settings," and copy the client ID under your name. Paste it onto your platform.
Note: This key is valid for 24 hours, so generate a new one daily.
