Step-by-Step Guide

Steps to Obtain API Key and Secret Key for ICICI Direct
  1. Visit the ICICI Direct API Portal and log in using your username and password.
  2. Verify your identity with the OTP and submit.
  3. Accept the Risk Disclosure.
  4. Click on the "Register an App" tab, then fill in the "App Name" field with "AlphaQuarkTest" or a name of your choice. Enter the "Redirect URL" as "https://ccxt.alphaquark.in/icici/auth-callback/test" and click "Submit". Please ensure that "redirect URL" entered correctly as mentioned above.
  5. Navigate to the "View Apps" tab and copy your API and Secret Key- enter these details in the screen.
